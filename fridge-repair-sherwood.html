Common Fridge Repair Problems in Sherwood

  • →Not cooling / warm fridge — Faulty compressor, refrigerant leak, or failed start relay — Alberta's cold winters can stress sealed systems. We diagnose before recommending compressor replacement.
  • →Excessive frost buildup — Failed defrost heater, thermostat, or timer causes ice accumulation on the evaporator coil — fridge runs constantly but doesn't cool.
  • →Ice maker not making ice — Failed water inlet valve, ice maker assembly, or temperature sensor. We carry parts for Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, and GE ice makers.
  • →Water dispenser not working — Frozen fill tube (common in Alberta winters when a door is left open), failed water valve, or broken dispenser switch.
  • →Loud clicking or humming noise — A clicking sound usually indicates the start relay attempting to start a failing compressor. Humming can be a faulty fan motor.
