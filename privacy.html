Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Nika Appliance Repair ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use nikaappliancerepair.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Booking details — name, phone number, address, appliance brand and issue, preferred service window. We use these to schedule and complete your repair.
  • Contact messages — anything you send via our forms or to info@nikaappliancerepair.com.
  • Standard analytics — anonymized page-view data so we can improve the site (no IP-level personal profiles).

How we use it

  • To dispatch a technician and complete the repair you requested.
  • To send a written quote, invoice, and follow-up reminders for warranty service.
  • To answer questions you send us.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your information to third parties.
  • We don't share your personal data with marketing networks.
  • We don't track you across other websites.

Third-party services we rely on

  • Online booking and dispatch software (Fixlify) processes your appointment request.
  • Payment processing is handled by a PCI-compliant payment provider; we never store full card numbers.

Your rights

Under Ontario and Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, and ask how it has been used. Email info@nikaappliancerepair.com with the subject "Privacy request".
