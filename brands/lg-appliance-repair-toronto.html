Common LG Appliance Problems We Fix Daily in Toronto

Our certified technicians have diagnosed and repaired over 2,500 LG appliances. Here are the most common issues we solve, with average repair times and what you can expect.

Why Is My LG Refrigerator Not Cooling?

Most Common Cause: Dirty condenser coils or faulty evaporator fan motor. In 75% of cases, we fix this issue within 1-2 hours.

⚠️ Warning: Delaying repair can cost you:

  • $300-$500 in spoiled food
  • $800-$1,200 compressor damage
  • $2,000+ for full replacement

💡 Quick Fix: Check if the back of your fridge feels hot. If yes, unplug it for 5 minutes and clean the coils with a vacuum. This solves 30% of cooling issues.

Other LG Refrigerator Issues We Fix:

  • Ice Maker Not Working - Usually a frozen water line (45-min fix)
  • Water Dispenser Problems - Often a clogged filter ($75-150)
  • Strange Noises - Typically fan motor issues (1-hour repair)
  • Error Codes (Er IF, Er FF) - Fan errors we fix same-day

Average Repair Cost: $200-$450 | Time: 1-2 hours

Why Won't My LG Washer Drain Water?

Most Common Cause: Clogged drain pump filter or kinked drain hose. The OE error code appears when water can't drain within 10 minutes.

💡 Quick Fix: Open the small door at the bottom front of your washer. Remove and clean the drain filter. This fixes 40% of drainage issues instantly.

Other LG Washer Problems We Solve:

  • UE Error (Unbalanced) - Suspension rod replacement ($150-300)
  • Not Spinning - Usually door latch or control board (1-hour fix)
  • LE Motor Error - Hall sensor issue (90-minute repair)
  • Door Won't Lock - Door latch assembly replacement ($175-250)

Average Repair Cost: $175-$350 | Time: 45-90 minutes

Why Is My LG Dryer Not Heating?

Most Common Cause: Blocked ventilation (60% of cases) or faulty heating element. The d80/d90 error means your vent is 80-90% blocked.

💡 Quick Fix: Check the outside vent while dryer is running. If little air comes out, your vent needs cleaning. This prevents fires and saves energy.

Other LG Dryer Issues We Repair:

  • Takes Too Long to Dry - Usually needs vent cleaning ($150-200)
  • Sensor Dry Not Working - Moisture sensor cleaning/replacement
  • Drum Not Turning - Belt or motor replacement (2-hour job)
  • Noisy Operation - Drum rollers or bearings ($200-350)

Average Repair Cost: $150-$400 | Time: 1-2 hours
