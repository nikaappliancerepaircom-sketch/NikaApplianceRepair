Questions

Frequently asked.

How much does appliance repair cost in Toronto? +
Diagnostic visits are $80, which is waived if you proceed with the repair. Standard repairs typically run $150–$280, while complex repairs involving compressors or control boards range from $280–$450. You receive a firm written quote before any work begins — the price never changes.
Do you offer same-day appliance repair? +
Yes. Same-day service is available Monday through Friday 8am–8pm, Saturday 9am–6pm, and Sunday 10am–5pm. Availability depends on your location and the time you call. We recommend calling before noon for the highest chance of same-day service.
What warranty do you provide on repairs? +
Every repair is covered by a 90-day warranty on both parts and labour. If the same issue recurs within 90 days of your repair, we will return and fix it at no charge. This warranty applies to all appliances and all brands we service.
Is it worth repairing or should I replace my appliance? +
A general rule: if the repair costs less than 50% of a new appliance's price and the appliance is under 10 years old, repair is almost always the better financial decision. Our technicians will give you an honest assessment and never recommend repairs that do not make economic sense.
Which brands do you repair? +
We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Bosch, Frigidaire, Maytag, KitchenAid, Electrolux, Kenmore, Miele, and Amana. If you do not see your brand listed, call us — we service virtually every residential brand sold in Canada.
Do your technicians carry parts with them? +
Yes. Our service vans are stocked with the most commonly needed OEM parts for all major brands. This is why 95% of repairs are completed in a single visit. In rare cases where a specific part must be ordered, we schedule a return visit as quickly as possible.
What areas of Toronto and the GTA do you serve? +
We serve all of Toronto including Scarborough, North York, and Etobicoke, as well as Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Ajax, and Pickering. If you are unsure whether we cover your area, call 437-524-1053 and we will confirm immediately.
