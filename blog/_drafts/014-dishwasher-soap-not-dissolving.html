Why Dishwasher Soap Isn't Dissolving

1. Water Temperature Too Low (40%)

Water below 120¬∞F doesn't dissolve pods or powder effectively.

DIY Fix: Set water heater to 120-140¬∞F, run hot water at sink before starting dishwasher.

2. Blocked Dispenser Door (30%)

Dishes block dispenser from opening, or dispenser spring fails.

DIY Fix: Rearrange dishes, clean dispenser door hinge, ensure nothing blocks opening.

3. Old or Wet Detergent Pods (20%)

Expired pods or pods exposed to moisture don't dissolve properly.

Fix: Use fresh pods, store in dry location, check expiration date.

4. Faulty Dispenser Mechanism

Dispenser door doesn't open at correct time during cycle.

Professional Fix: Dispenser replacement ($100-$180).
