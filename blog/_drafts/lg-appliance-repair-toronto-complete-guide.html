Common Issues with LG Appliances

LG Refrigerator Problems

  • Compressor failure: LG's linear compressors are efficient but can fail. Look for IF error code
  • Ice maker troubles: LG's craft ice makers require specific water pressure and temperature
  • Cooling fan noise: Linear compressor units have distinct sound profiles when fans fail
  • InstaView panel issues: Knock-to-view feature can stop responding

LG Washing Machine Issues

  • UE/uE error: Unbalanced load detected - may indicate worn shocks or bearings
  • OE error: Drain pump blockage or failure common after 5-6 years
  • LE error: Motor overload, often from worn carbon brushes or hall sensor
  • Door lock failure: Front-loaders won't start if lock mechanism fails

LG Dryer Problems

  • d80/d90/d95 codes: Exhaust restriction warnings specific to LG
  • No heat: Flow sensor or heating element failure
  • Sensor dry malfunction: Moisture sensors need cleaning or replacement
  • Drum not turning: Belt or motor issue common after 7+ years

LG Dishwasher Issues

  • AE leak error: Base pan sensor triggered - door seal or pump leak
  • IE fill error: Inlet valve failure or low water pressure
  • Poor cleaning: Spray arm bearing wear affects water distribution

Before Calling for LG Repair

Try LG Smart Diagnosis

Most LG appliances have Smart Diagnosis built in:

  1. Call LG support: 1-888-542-2623
  2. Select Smart Diagnosis option
  3. Hold phone speaker to appliance as instructed
  4. The tones transmitted provide diagnostic data

LG Refrigerator Quick Fixes

  • Not cooling: Check that vents inside aren't blocked by food
  • Ice maker not working: Verify water line isn't frozen (hair dryer method)
  • Noisy operation: Ensure unit is level and has clearance behind

LG Washer Quick Fixes

  • Won't start: Check child lock isn't activated (3-second button hold)
  • UE error: Redistribute load evenly, check for single heavy items
  • Vibration: Verify all shipping bolts removed, check leveling feet

LG Dryer Quick Fixes

  • d80/d90/d95: Clean entire exhaust path - these codes mean airflow restriction
  • No heat: Check that both circuit breakers are on (240V requires two)
  • Won't start: Ensure door latches completely, check door switch

Reset Procedure (All LG Appliances)

  1. Turn off and unplug appliance
  2. Wait 5-10 minutes
  3. Plug back in and power on
  4. Run a test cycle to check if error clears

When to Call a Professional

While some appliance issues can be resolved with DIY troubleshooting, certain situations require professional expertise:

  • Electrical problems: Any repair involving wiring, control boards, or high-voltage components
  • Gas appliance issues: All repairs on gas appliances should be done by licensed technicians
  • Refrigerant systems: Sealed system repairs require EPA certification
  • Persistent problems: Issues that return after DIY attempts indicate deeper problems
  • Under warranty: DIY repairs can void manufacturer coverage
  • Water damage risk: Leak repairs need proper diagnosis to prevent flood damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LG have good warranty coverage?

LG offers strong warranties: 1 year parts/labor standard, 10 years on Direct Drive motors (washers), and 10 years on Linear Compressors (refrigerators). Always check your warranty status before paying for repairs - compressor and motor replacements are often covered.

Why are LG washer bearing repairs so expensive?

LG front-load washers have sealed bearing assemblies integrated with the outer tub. Replacing bearings requires splitting the tub, which is labor-intensive (2-3 hours). The parts themselves are reasonably priced but labor is significant.

Are LG appliances harder to repair than other brands?

LG uses more electronic controls and sensors than some competitors, which requires diagnostic equipment. However, LG parts are readily available and their engineering is logical once you understand their systems. Trained technicians can repair LG efficiently.

My LG fridge compressor failed at 4 years - is that covered?

Yes! LG's Linear Compressor warranty is 10 years. Contact us with your model and serial number, and we'll verify warranty coverage before proceeding. You may only be responsible for labor, not the compressor itself.

Can you repair LG's smart features and Wi-Fi connectivity?

We diagnose and repair the physical components that enable smart features. Wi-Fi connectivity issues are often software-related - try factory reset, app reinstall, or router troubleshooting first. If a sensor or control board causes the issue, we can replace those.
