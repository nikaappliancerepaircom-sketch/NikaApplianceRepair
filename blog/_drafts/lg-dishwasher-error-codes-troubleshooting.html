LG Dishwasher Error Codes: Complete Troubleshooting Guide

December 06, 2025 Expert Team

LG dishwashers display specific error codes when something goes wrong. Understanding these codes can save you time and help you decide whether it's a DIY fix or time to call a professional. This comprehensive guide covers every LG dishwasher error code, what causes each one, and exactly how to resolve it.

At Nika Appliance Repair, we've diagnosed over 2,400 LG dishwashers across Toronto since 2017. Our technicians are LG-certified and keep common replacement parts on hand. Let's decode those error messages together.

LG Dishwasher Error Code Directory

AE Error - Leak Detected

The AE code means water has reached the base pan sensor. This is LG's flood protection system.

  • Door seal damage: Check rubber gasket for cracks or debris
  • Spray arm crack: Pressurized water can escape through damaged arms
  • Tub perforation: Rare but serious - requires professional assessment
  • Pump seal failure: Internal leak at the wash pump

OE Error - Drain Failure

Your LG can't evacuate water within the expected timeframe.

  • Filter blockage: Food particles clogging the cylindrical filter
  • Drain hose kink: Flexible hose compressed behind the unit
  • Garbage disposal knockout plug: New installations often miss this step
  • Drain pump motor failure: Motor windings can burn out after 5-7 years

IE Error - Fill Timeout

The dishwasher isn't receiving water fast enough.

  • Supply valve closed: Check under-sink shutoff is fully open
  • Inlet valve screen clogged: Toronto's hard water deposits minerals here
  • Water inlet valve failure: Solenoid no longer opens properly
  • Low water pressure: Below 20 PSI causes fill timeouts

HE Error - Heater Malfunction

Water isn't reaching proper temperature for sanitization.

  • Heating element burnout: Common after 6-8 years of use
  • Thermistor fault: Temperature sensor sending wrong readings
  • Control board relay: Board can't activate the heater circuit

FE Error - Motor Problem

The wash motor or circulation system has failed.

  • Motor overload: Debris jamming the impeller causes overheating
  • Capacitor failure: Motor won't start without working capacitor
  • Wiring harness damage: Connections corroded by moisture

Safe DIY Solutions for LG Error Codes

Clearing AE (Leak) Error

  1. Power off and unplug the dishwasher
  2. Pull unit forward carefully to access the base
  3. Use towels to absorb any pooled water in the base pan
  4. Inspect door gasket for food debris or damage
  5. Check spray arm connections for looseness
  6. Let base pan dry completely (use fan if needed)
  7. Restore power and run test cycle

Resolving OE (Drain) Error

  1. Remove lower rack and access cylindrical filter
  2. Twist filter counterclockwise and lift out
  3. Rinse under hot water, scrubbing with soft brush
  4. Check filter cavity for debris, remove with paper towel
  5. Inspect drain hose under sink for kinks
  6. If connected to garbage disposal, verify knockout removed
  7. Reinstall filter and run Rinse cycle

Fixing IE (Fill) Error

  1. Confirm hot water supply valve fully open under sink
  2. Turn off water and disconnect supply line from dishwasher
  3. Check inlet screen for mineral buildup (mesh filter at valve)
  4. Clean screen with vinegar soak or replace if damaged
  5. Reconnect supply line and restore water
  6. Run hot water at kitchen sink first, then start dishwasher

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my LG dishwasher keep showing AE error after drying the base?

The float switch in the base pan may be stuck. Remove the base access panel, locate the white float mechanism, and ensure it moves freely up and down. If it's stuck in the up position, it continuously signals a leak even when dry.

My LG shows OE but water seems to drain - what's wrong?

The drain cycle has a specific time limit. If water drains slowly due to partial blockage, the dishwasher times out and shows OE. Check for buildup in the drain hose connection at the garbage disposal or air gap.

How do I find my LG dishwasher model number for parts?

Open the door and look on the left side panel frame - there's a silver sticker with model and serial numbers. You can also find it in the LG ThinQ app if your unit is connected. Model numbers start with LDF, LDT, or LDP.

Can I run my LG dishwasher with an active error code?

It's not recommended. Error codes indicate protective shutdowns. Running cycles with active errors can cause additional damage. Clear the error first, or have a technician diagnose before continued use.

Are LG dishwasher error codes the same across all models?

Yes, LG uses consistent error codes across their dishwasher lineup. Whether you have a front-control or top-control model, AE means leak, OE means drain issue, IE means fill problem, and so on.
