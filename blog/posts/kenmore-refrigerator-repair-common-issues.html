Kenmore Refrigerator Repair: Common Issues & Solutions

December 12, 2025 Expert Team

Kenmore refrigerators have been household staples for decades, offering solid performance at reasonable prices. But as Sears declined, many owners wonder who services Kenmore now and whether repairs are worthwhile. This guide covers common Kenmore fridge problems and repair options in the post-Sears era.

Common Kenmore Refrigerator Problems

Who Actually Makes Kenmore?

Understanding your Kenmore's true manufacturer helps with repairs:

  • Model starts with 106: Built by Whirlpool - most common
  • Model starts with 795: Built by LG - premium models
  • Model starts with 253: Built by Frigidaire/Electrolux
  • Model starts with 596: Built by Whirlpool/Amana

Not Cooling Issues

  • Dirty condenser coils: Kenmore models often have hard-to-reach coils that collect dust
  • Evaporator fan failure: Cold air doesn't circulate from freezer to fridge
  • Start relay failure: Compressor clicks but won't run
  • Sealed system leak: Refrigerant escapes, cooling capacity drops

Ice Maker Problems (LG-built models)

  • Frozen water line: Same issue as LG branded units - fill tube freezes
  • Ice maker assembly failure: Motor or thermostat in the maker itself
  • Auger motor burnout: Ice won't dispense even though it's being made

Water Leaking

  • Clogged defrost drain: Ice builds up, melts, water pools under crisper drawers
  • Water filter housing crack: Pressurized water escapes at filter
  • Door gasket failure: Condensation forms and drips

Noise Complaints

  • Evaporator fan blade: Ice buildup causes scraping sound
  • Condenser fan motor: Worn bearings create buzzing or humming
  • Compressor vibration: Mounting grommets worn, compressor vibrates

DIY Fixes for Kenmore Refrigerators

Clean Condenser Coils

  1. Unplug refrigerator
  2. Locate coils - usually behind base grille (front) or on back
  3. Remove base grille (pull straight out)
  4. Use condenser coil brush (long, flexible brush)
  5. Vacuum loosened dust and debris
  6. Replace grille and restore power
  7. Repeat every 6-12 months

Clear Clogged Defrost Drain

  1. Remove crisper drawers and shelves for access
  2. Locate drain hole (back wall of fridge compartment, center bottom)
  3. Mix 1 cup hot water with 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Use turkey baster to flush solution through drain
  5. If blocked, use pipe cleaner or flexible wire to clear ice
  6. Flush again with warm water

Reset After Power Issues

  1. Unplug refrigerator for 5 minutes
  2. Plug back in
  3. Listen for compressor startup (within 1-2 minutes)
  4. Allow 24 hours to reach target temperature

Door Seal Test

  • Close door on dollar bill, then try to pull it out
  • Should feel resistance - if bill slides easily, seal is worn
  • Test multiple spots around door perimeter
  • Clean gasket with warm soapy water if sticky or dirty

When to Call a Professional

While some refrigerator issues can be resolved with DIY troubleshooting, certain situations require professional expertise:

  • Electrical problems: Any repair involving wiring, control boards, or high-voltage components
  • Gas appliance issues: All repairs on gas appliances should be done by licensed technicians
  • Refrigerant systems: Sealed system repairs require EPA certification
  • Persistent problems: Issues that return after DIY attempts indicate deeper problems
  • Under warranty: DIY repairs can void manufacturer coverage
  • Water damage risk: Leak repairs need proper diagnosis to prevent flood damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Kenmore refrigerators still be repaired now that Sears is gone?

Absolutely. Kenmore appliances are built by major manufacturers (LG, Whirlpool, Frigidaire) who still make parts. Independent repair companies like ours can source genuine or compatible parts and service any Kenmore model. Parts availability hasn't changed.

My Kenmore 795 model has ice maker problems - is that related to the LG class action?

Yes, Kenmore 795 models are built by LG and share the same ice maker design issues. The frozen fill tube problem affects these units too. We install the same fill tube heater kit fix that works on LG branded refrigerators.

How do I know if my old Kenmore refrigerator is worth repairing?

For refrigerators under 10 years old, most repairs make sense. Compressor replacement on units over 12-15 years old may not be cost-effective since other components are likely near end of life too. We provide honest assessments based on repair cost vs. unit age and condition.

Will any technician work on Kenmore appliances?

Most independent appliance repair companies service Kenmore. The key is finding someone who knows the actual manufacturer. When you call us, we ask for your model number first so we can prepare with the correct parts and diagnostic approach.

Where do I find replacement parts for my Kenmore refrigerator?

Parts are available through appliance parts distributors, online retailers, and directly from the actual manufacturer. Your Kenmore model number cross-references to the original maker's part numbers. We handle parts sourcing as part of our repair service.
