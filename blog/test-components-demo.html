How do I prevent hard water damage to my dishwasher in Toronto?

To prevent hard water damage in Toronto (200-300 ppm), use rinse aid monthly, clean the filter weekly, run monthly descaling cycles with white vinegar, and use dishwasher salt if your model has a softener compartment. This costs $10-50/month vs $300-600 for repairs.
