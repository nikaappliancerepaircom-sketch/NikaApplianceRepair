Service Area Questions

What areas does Nika Appliance Repair service?

We provide professional appliance repair throughout the Greater Toronto Area with a 50km service radius from downtown Toronto. This includes all major cities: Toronto (Downtown, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke), Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Ajax, Pickering, Oshawa, and Milton. We serve hundreds of neighborhoods across these cities with fast response times.

How quickly can you get to my location?

Response times vary by location. Toronto core areas typically receive service within 30-60 minutes. Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, and Vaughan usually have 45-90 minute response times. Oakville, Burlington, and Durham Region (Ajax, Pickering, Oshawa) typically see 90-120 minute responses. We offer same-day service across all our service areas and can often accommodate emergency requests.

Do you charge extra for certain neighborhoods or cities?

Our service call fee is consistent across all GTA locations within our 50km service radius. We believe in transparent, upfront pricing regardless of whether you're in downtown Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, or any other city we serve. There are no hidden travel fees or neighborhood surcharges. You'll know the cost before we begin any repair work.

Can you service my neighborhood specifically?

Yes! We provide comprehensive coverage throughout all GTA neighborhoods. Whether you're in Port Credit, Bramalea, Unionville, Woodbridge, Glen Abbey, Aldershot, or any other community within our service area, we have experienced technicians ready to help. Call us at 437-747-6737 to confirm service availability in your specific neighborhood.

Do you offer weekend and evening service in all areas?

Yes, we provide 7-day-a-week service across all GTA locations we serve. Weekend appointments are available throughout Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, and Durham Region. Evening appointments can also be arranged in most areas. We understand appliance emergencies don't only happen during business hours.

What if I'm just outside your main service area?

We may still be able to help! While our primary 50km service radius covers most of the GTA, we occasionally travel to nearby areas. Contact us at 437-747-6737 with your location, and we'll let you know if we can accommodate your repair needs. We're committed to helping as many GTA residents as possible.
