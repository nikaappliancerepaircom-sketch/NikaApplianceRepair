07 — FAQ
Common Questions
Questions,
Answered.
Do you offer same-day appliance repair?
Yes. In most cases we can dispatch a technician the same day you call. We serve Toronto and the GTA 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays. Call 437-524-1053 to check availability.
What brands do you service?
We repair all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, GE, Maytag, KitchenAid, Electrolux, Frigidaire, Miele, Sub-Zero, and many more. If you own it, we can fix it.
Do you charge a diagnostic fee?
We charge a service call fee to come out and diagnose the problem. This fee is waived or applied toward the repair cost when you proceed with the fix. You always get a quote before any work begins.
Are your repairs warrantied?
Yes. All our parts and labour are backed by a warranty. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we return at no additional charge. We stand behind our work completely.
How much does appliance repair cost?
Repair costs vary by appliance type, brand, and the nature of the fault. We provide a transparent flat-rate quote after diagnosis — no hourly rates, no surprise charges. Most common repairs range from $120 to $350.
Should I repair or replace my appliance?
Our technicians give you an honest assessment. The general rule: if the repair cost is less than 50% of a new appliance's price and the appliance is under 10 years old, repair almost always wins economically.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve the full Greater Toronto Area: Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Thornhill, Ajax, Pickering, and more.
Do you carry parts on the truck?
Yes. Our service vehicles are stocked with OEM and manufacturer-approved parts for the most common failures across all major brands. This allows us to complete most repairs in a single visit — no waiting for parts to arrive.
