About Sarah

Sarah Chen is one of Toronto's most experienced and trusted refrigerator repair specialists, with over 12 years of hands-on experience diagnosing and repairing all major cooling appliance brands.

Sarah has built her reputation on her exceptional ability to quickly diagnose complex cooling system failures that other technicians might miss. Her deep understanding of refrigeration technology, combined with manufacturer certifications from Samsung, LG, and Whirlpool, allows her to tackle even the most challenging repair scenarios.

What sets Sarah apart is her 98% first-visit fix rate – one of the highest in the industry. This impressive success rate comes from her methodical diagnostic approach, extensive parts inventory, and commitment to staying current with the latest refrigerator technologies. Whether it's a simple thermostat issue or a complex compressor failure, Sarah has the expertise to get your refrigerator running efficiently again.

Throughout her 12-year career, Sarah has completed over 1,800 successful repairs across Toronto and the GTA. She takes pride in not just fixing appliances, but educating her customers about proper maintenance and care to extend the life of their refrigerators.
