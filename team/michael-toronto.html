About Michael

Michael Toronto is Toronto's go-to dishwasher repair specialist, with 10 years of focused expertise in diagnosing and resolving complex dishwasher issues, particularly those related to Toronto's challenging hard water conditions.

What makes Michael unique is his deep understanding of how Toronto's hard water affects dishwasher performance. Living and working in the GTA for over a decade, he's become an expert at identifying and solving hard water-related problems that many other technicians overlook. From mineral buildup to drainage issues caused by water hardness, Michael has seen it all and knows exactly how to fix it.

As a Bosch and KitchenAid certified technician, Michael brings manufacturer-level expertise to every repair. His 96% success rate reflects his methodical diagnostic approach and commitment to getting dishwashers running properly the first time. He carries an extensive inventory of common parts and knows which components are most affected by hard water conditions.

Beyond repairs, Michael is passionate about educating Toronto homeowners on proper dishwasher maintenance in hard water areas. He regularly shares tips on water softener settings, rinse aid usage, and cleaning schedules that can prevent many common dishwasher problems before they start.

Having completed over 1,500 successful repairs across the GTA, Michael has built a reputation for honesty, reliability, and expertise. His customers appreciate his clear explanations, transparent pricing, and ability to solve even the toughest dishwasher challenges.
