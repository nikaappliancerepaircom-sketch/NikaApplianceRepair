About David

David Martinez is Toronto's premier oven and stove repair specialist, with 11 years of focused expertise in diagnosing and repairing all types of cooking appliances, from standard home ranges to high-end professional-grade equipment.

What distinguishes David in the appliance repair field is his exceptional expertise with premium brands. As a Wolf, Viking, and Thermador certified technician, he brings manufacturer-level knowledge to repairs of these sophisticated cooking appliances. Whether you have a basic electric range or a professional-grade dual-fuel system, David has the training and experience to service it properly.

David's approach to repairs is methodical and thorough. He understands that cooking appliances are often the heart of the home, and a malfunctioning oven or stove can disrupt daily life significantly. His diagnostic process systematically tests each component – from heating elements and igniters to thermostats and control boards – to identify the exact cause of the problem before beginning repairs.

With expertise in both gas and electric systems, David can handle any cooking appliance challenge. From gas igniter replacements and burner valve repairs to heating element failures and control board diagnostics, his comprehensive knowledge ensures that your oven or stove receives expert care. His 96% first-visit fix rate reflects his thorough preparation and extensive parts inventory.

Safety is paramount when working with cooking appliances, particularly gas systems. David follows strict safety protocols and ensures all repairs meet or exceed manufacturer specifications and local building codes. His attention to detail and commitment to quality workmanship give Toronto homeowners peace of mind that their cooking appliances are operating safely and efficiently.

Over his 11-year career, David has completed more than 1,600 successful repairs across Toronto and the GTA. His customers appreciate his professionalism, clear communication, and ability to explain complex repairs in understandable terms. Whether it's a simple thermostat replacement or a complex control board repair, David approaches every job with the same level of expertise and dedication.
