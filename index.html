Most appliances are worth repairing if they're less than 10 years old and the repair cost is less than 50% of replacement cost. Our technicians provide honest assessments and will tell you if replacement makes more sense.

Generally, repair if: the appliance is less than 8 years old, repair costs less than 50% of new, or it's a high-end model. Replace if: it's over 15 years old, repairs are frequent, or energy efficiency is poor.
