FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Yes! We offer same-day service across Toronto and the GTA for most appliance repairs. Call us before noon and we'll do our best to have a technician at your door that day. Availability depends on your area and schedule.

Every repair is different, so we always provide a free diagnosis and upfront quote before any work starts. Minor repairs typically range from $80–$150. Major component replacements vary. You'll always know the exact cost before we proceed — no surprises.

All our repairs come with a 90-day parts and labour warranty. If the same issue recurs within 90 days of your repair, we return and fix it at no additional charge. We stand behind every job we do.

Yes, we use original manufacturer (OEM) parts whenever possible. This ensures the best compatibility, longevity, and performance for your appliance. We carry a wide inventory of parts for all major brands in our service vehicles.

As a general rule, if the repair cost is less than 50% of the replacement cost of a comparable new appliance and the appliance is under 10 years old, repairing is usually the smarter financial choice. Our technician will give you an honest recommendation after the free diagnosis.
