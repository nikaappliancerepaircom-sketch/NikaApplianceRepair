Our Story

Founded in 2017, Nika Appliance Repair started with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and affordable appliance repair services to families and businesses across the Greater Toronto Area.

What began as a one-person operation has grown into a team of certified technicians serving thousands of satisfied customers. We've repaired over 10,000 appliances and built a reputation for excellence through our commitment to quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

We're a professional appliance repair company dedicated to keeping your home running smoothly. Every repair comes with a 90-day warranty because we stand behind our work.
