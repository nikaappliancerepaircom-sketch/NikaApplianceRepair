Common Stove Problems We Fix

⭐ Rated 4.9/5 by 5,200+ Toronto homeowners. From burners not heating to igniters clicking continuously, our expert stove repair technicians diagnose and fix all stove issues fast. Don't let a broken stove stop you from cooking - we're here to help.

⚠️

Burners Not Heating

Your gas burners won't ignite or electric burners stay cold. For gas stoves, this usually means faulty igniters, clogged burner ports, or a bad spark module. Electric stoves typically have broken infinite switches or burned-out elements. We diagnose which component failed and replace it with genuine OEM parts same-day.

⚠️

Uneven Heating Issues

Some burners heat properly while others barely warm up, or the flame is weak and yellow instead of strong blue. This indicates clogged burner orifices on gas stoves or failing infinite switches on electric models. We clean all ports thoroughly or replace defective switches to restore even heat distribution across all burners.

⚠️

Igniter Keeps Clicking

The igniter sparks continuously even after the burner is lit or when no burners are on. This is caused by moisture in the igniter switch, a faulty spark module, or a grounding issue. The constant clicking drains power and can damage the spark module. We identify and fix the root cause quickly.

⚠️

Gas Smell Problem

You smell gas when burners are off or during operation. This serious safety hazard requires immediate attention. Common causes include loose connections, faulty gas valves, or cracked burner manifolds. We perform thorough leak detection, tighten all connections, and replace any damaged gas components to ensure your family's safety.

⚠️

Electric Element Broken

One or more electric coil elements won't heat up at all or have visible damage like breaks or blistering. The element itself has burned out, or the receptacle that connects it has corroded. We carry replacement elements and receptacles for all major brands, ensuring your cooktop heats properly again.

⚠️

Control Board Failure

The digital display is blank, buttons don't respond, or error codes appear constantly. The electronic control board has failed due to power surges or component wear. This complex repair requires expertise to diagnose correctly. We test the board thoroughly and replace it with a factory-spec control board if needed.

Our Stove Repair Process

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.9/5 rating | 5,200+ repairs | 90-day warranty

When your stove stops working, meal preparation becomes impossible. That's why we offer same-day stove repair service across Toronto and the GTA. Our certified stove repair technicians arrive in fully-stocked service trucks. We carry the most common stove parts for all major brands including igniters, infinite switches, and burner elements.

We begin with a thorough 15-minute diagnostic covering both gas and electric systems. This includes testing igniters, checking burner orifices, inspecting infinite switches, and examining gas valves. We also test the spark module and control board. This comprehensive inspection pinpoints the exact problem quickly.

For gas stoves, we check each igniter's spark strength and timing. Burner caps get inspected for proper seating. We examine all gas connections for leaks and test the safety valves. The spark module is tested to ensure it generates proper voltage to all igniters.

Electric stoves require different diagnostics. We test each infinite switch with a multimeter to verify proper resistance. Burner elements are inspected for breaks or damage. Receptacles that connect elements to the stove get checked for burns or corrosion. Wiring is examined for loose connections.

Before starting repairs, you receive an upfront quote covering all parts and labor. No hidden fees or surprise charges ever. Most stove repairs range from $150 to $450 depending on the issue. Once you approve, we proceed immediately with the repair.

We exclusively use genuine OEM parts or premium equivalents meeting manufacturer specifications. Cheap aftermarket igniters fail within months - we never use them. Quality matters because your stove must work reliably every single day.

Our parts inventory includes surface burner igniters, spark modules, infinite switches, burner elements, receptacles, gas safety valves, and control boards. We stock parts for GE, Whirlpool, Frigidaire, Samsung, LG, Bosch, and all other major brands.

Most stove repairs complete within 45 minutes to 1.5 hours on the same visit. Complex issues like control board replacement might take 2 hours. We test every burner after repair to confirm proper operation before leaving.
