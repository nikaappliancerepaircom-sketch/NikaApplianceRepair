Common Freezer Problems We Fix

⭐ Rated 4.9/5 by 5,200+ Toronto homeowners. From not freezing to excessive frost buildup, our expert freezer repair technicians diagnose and fix all freezer issues fast. Don't let thawing food cost you hundreds - we're here to help.

⚠️

Freezer Not Freezing or Too Warm

Your freezer isn't maintaining freezing temperatures and food is thawing or getting soft. This could be a failed compressor, faulty thermostat, or low refrigerant level. We diagnose the exact cause within 15 minutes and restore proper freezing temperatures fast. Most repairs are completed same-day with parts we carry on our trucks.

⚠️

Excessive Frost or Ice Buildup

Heavy frost accumulates inside your freezer, covering walls and food items. This indicates a defrost system failure - the defrost timer, heater, or thermostat has stopped working properly. Left unfixed, frost reduces storage space and cooling efficiency. We repair all defrost components and clear ice buildup completely.

⚠️

Freezer Won't Run or Start

Your freezer isn't running at all and everything is thawing. The compressor relay, start capacitor, or control board has likely failed. Sometimes it's a simple electrical issue like a tripped circuit breaker. Our technicians test all components systematically to identify and fix the problem quickly before all your food spoils.

⚠️

Loud or Unusual Noises

Your freezer makes grinding, buzzing, clicking, or humming sounds. These noises indicate worn compressor bearings, a failing evaporator fan motor, or loose internal components. Ignoring strange sounds leads to complete breakdowns. We pinpoint the noise source and replace failing components to restore quiet operation and prevent costly failures.

⚠️

Door Seal Not Working

The freezer door doesn't seal tightly, letting warm air in and cold air out. A worn or damaged door gasket is the usual culprit. This forces your compressor to run constantly, wastes energy, and causes frost buildup. We replace door gaskets with perfect-fit OEM seals that restore airtight closure and reduce energy costs.

⚠️

Ice Buildup on Freezer Floor

You're finding ice accumulating at the bottom of your freezer compartment. This happens when the defrost drain becomes clogged with food particles or ice. Water from defrost cycles can't drain properly and refreezes. We clear blocked drain tubes, clean drain pans, and ensure proper water flow to eliminate ice buildup issues.
