Washer Not Spinning or Draining

Your clothes come out soaking wet because the washer won't spin or water won't drain. This is usually a broken lid switch, worn drive belt, faulty pump, or clogged drain hose. We test all spin components, replace the faulty part, and clear any blockages. Most spin and drain issues are resolved same-day with parts readily available.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Washer Leaking Water

Water pools around your washing machine during or after cycles. Common causes include worn door seals, loose hose connections, damaged tub seals, or a cracked drain pump. We inspect all seals and hoses, tighten connections, and replace damaged components. Leak repairs prevent water damage to your floors and belongings.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Not Filling with Water

Cycle starts but no water enters the washing machine or it fills extremely slowly. This indicates a faulty inlet valve, clogged inlet screens, or low water pressure. We test water flow, clean screens, replace inlet valves, and check water supply lines. Water fill problems are typically fixed within an hour.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Won't Start or No Power

Pressing start does nothing or the control panel is completely dead. This could be a blown thermal fuse, faulty door lock, bad control board, or power supply issue. We test all electrical components, check circuit connections, and replace failed parts. Most electrical issues are diagnosed and fixed same-visit.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Loud Noises During Operation

Grinding, banging, or squealing sounds during wash or spin cycles. Worn tub bearings, broken motor coupling, loose drum, or foreign objects stuck in the pump are typical causes. We remove obstructions, replace worn bearings, and fix drum assemblies. Your washer will run smoothly and quietly again.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Excessive Vibration or Shaking

Washer shakes violently and walks across the floor during spin cycle. This is usually caused by an unbalanced load, worn shock absorbers, broken suspension springs, or unlevel installation. We balance the washer, replace suspension components, adjust leveling feet, and test spin stability. Excessive vibration is stopped quickly.
