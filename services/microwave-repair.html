Common Microwave Problems We Fix

⭐ Rated 4.9/5 by 5,200+ Toronto homeowners. From microwaves not heating to sparking inside, our expert microwave repair technicians diagnose and fix all microwave issues fast. Don't let a broken microwave disrupt your kitchen routine - we're here to help.

⚠️

Not Heating Food

Is your microwave running but not heating food? This common issue is typically caused by a failed magnetron (the component that generates microwaves), blown high-voltage diode, faulty high-voltage capacitor, or defective door switch preventing the magnetron from operating. The magnetron is especially prone to failure in microwaves over 5 years old. Our technicians safely test all high-voltage components and replace the failed parts to restore heating function.

⚠️

Sparking Inside Microwave

Seeing sparks or arcing inside your microwave? This dangerous issue is usually caused by a burned waveguide cover, damaged interior paint exposing metal, food debris on the interior walls, or a failing magnetron antenna. Continued use with sparking can cause fire hazards and further damage. We inspect the waveguide cover, interior cavity, and magnetron assembly to identify the source of arcing and replace damaged components.

⚠️

Turntable Not Spinning

A non-rotating turntable leads to uneven heating since food doesn't rotate through the microwave energy pattern. This problem is typically caused by a failed turntable motor, broken drive coupler connecting the motor to the glass tray, damaged support roller wheels, or misaligned turntable guide. We test the turntable motor, inspect the drive mechanism, replace worn couplers and rollers, and ensure smooth rotation for even heating.

⚠️

Door Won't Close or Latch

A microwave door that won't close properly or latch is a safety issue since the microwave won't operate without proper door closure. This is typically caused by broken door latches, worn door springs, bent door frame, misaligned door catch, or failed door switches. The door interlock system has multiple safety switches that must all engage. We replace broken latches and switches, adjust door alignment, and ensure all safety mechanisms function properly.

⚠️

Control Panel Not Working

An unresponsive or malfunctioning control panel prevents you from operating the microwave. This issue can be caused by a failed touchpad membrane, faulty control board, loose wire connections, or damaged keypad interface. Sometimes specific buttons stop working while others function normally. We test the touchpad continuity, inspect control board connections, diagnose electronic failures, and replace the touchpad or control board as needed.

⚠️

Makes Loud Humming Noise

Unusual loud humming, buzzing, or grinding noises from your microwave indicate mechanical or electrical component problems. A loud humming can point to a failing magnetron, high-voltage transformer issues, worn turntable motor bearings, or cooling fan problems. Grinding noises often indicate turntable motor or coupler failure. We diagnose the noise source, test electrical components, inspect mechanical parts, and replace noisy or failing components to restore quiet operation.

Our Microwave Repair Process

When your microwave stops working - whether it's not heating food, sparking inside, or the control panel is unresponsive - you need expert microwave repair service fast. Our microwave repair process starts with a comprehensive diagnostic inspection using specialized safety equipment. We test the magnetron (microwave generator), high-voltage diode, high-voltage capacitor, high-voltage transformer, door switches (primary, secondary, and monitor), control board, touchpad, turntable motor, and cooling fan. After repairs, we perform thorough safety checks including testing all door switches, verifying proper magnetron operation, checking high-voltage component function, testing the turntable system, and running heating cycles to ensure safe, proper operation.
