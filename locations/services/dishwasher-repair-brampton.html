Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Brampton

From not cleaning dishes to leaks and drainage failures, our Brampton technicians fix all dishwasher problems — usually in a single visit.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning Dishes

Dishes come out still dirty. Clogged spray arms, a worn wash pump, low water pressure, or a blocked filter are typical causes — all accelerated by heavy daily use in large Brampton households. We clean and replace components to restore full cleaning performance.

Dishwasher Not Draining

Standing water left at the bottom after a cycle. A clogged drain hose, blocked filter, or failed drain pump is usually responsible. We clear blockages and replace failed pumps for complete drainage restoration.

Dishwasher Leaking Water

Water on the kitchen floor or inside the cabinet below. A worn door gasket, loose spray arm, cracked pump housing, or damaged inlet valve connection causes leaks. We identify the leak source and repair it before cabinet damage occurs.

Dishwasher Won't Start

No response when you press start. A faulty door latch, blown thermal fuse, failed control board, or tripped circuit breaker can prevent startup. We trace the electrical fault and replace the failed component.

Dishes Not Drying

Dishes are wet at the end of the cycle. A burned-out heating element, failed vent, or faulty rinse aid dispenser causes poor drying results. We restore proper drying function so dishes come out ready to put away.

Dishwasher Making Loud Noises

Grinding, rattling, or humming during operation. A worn wash pump motor, debris caught in the pump impeller, or loose spray arm are common causes. We inspect the full pump and spray system and eliminate the noise source.
