Common Washer Problems We Fix in Vaughan

Premium washers in Vaughan homes require specialist diagnosis. Our technicians handle everything from Miele error codes to Bosch door seal replacements.

Washer Not Spinning

Clothes come out soaking wet after a full cycle. On Miele and Bosch front-loaders, this often indicates a worn drum bearing, failing motor brush, or control board issue. We diagnose at the component level and replace only what's needed.

Washer Leaking Water

Premium Vaughan kitchens and laundry rooms feature expensive flooring — a leaking washer is urgent. Door boot seal failure is the most common cause on European front-loaders. We carry OEM Miele and Bosch seals for same-day repair.

Error Codes and Fault Displays

Miele, Bosch, and LG washers display detailed error codes when a fault occurs. Our technicians use manufacturer-level diagnostic tools to interpret these codes accurately and address the root cause, not just clear the display.

Won't Drain or Won't Fill

A blocked filter, kinked hose, or failed pump prevents drainage. A faulty pressure switch or water inlet valve prevents filling. Both issues are straightforward repairs our Vaughan technicians resolve in a single visit.

Excessive Vibration or Noise

Loud banging during spin in Vaughan's open-plan homes is disruptive. Worn shock absorbers, damaged drum bearings, or loose counterbalances cause this. We restore quiet, stable operation — particularly important in homes with neighbouring suites or open layouts.

Door Won't Open or Lock Fails

A front-load washer that won't unlock at end of cycle, or refuses to lock and start, typically has a failed door interlock mechanism. We replace door latch assemblies for all brands with OEM components and a 90-day warranty.
