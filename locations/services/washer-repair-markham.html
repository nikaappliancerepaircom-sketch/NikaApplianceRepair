Common Washer Problems We Fix in Markham

From smart appliance error codes to a washer that simply won't drain, our Markham technicians diagnose and fix most faults in a single visit.

Samsung / LG Error Codes

Smart washers in Markham homes often display fault codes like 4E, UE, LE, or dE before stopping mid-cycle. Our technicians carry brand-specific diagnostic tools to interpret codes and isolate the root cause — whether it's a faulty sensor, control board, or door latch.

Washer Won't Drain

Water stays in the drum after the cycle. A clogged pump filter, failed drain pump motor, or kinked hose are typical causes. York Region water hardness can deposit scale in pumps over time. Fixed same-day for $150-$280.

Won't Spin or Agitate

Clothes come out soaking wet because the drum won't spin. Usually a worn drive belt, broken coupling, lid switch fault, or inverter motor issue on newer direct-drive Samsung and LG models. We replace failed components on-site.

Leaking Water

Water on the laundry room floor can cause serious damage in Markham's many finished basements. Door boot seals on front-loaders, cracked hoses, and worn pump seals are common causes. We identify the source and seal the leak same-day.

Excessive Vibration and Noise

Banging or vibration during spin is especially problematic in Markham's semi-detached and townhome communities. Worn drum bearings, broken shock absorbers, or unlevel feet are typical causes. We restore quiet, smooth operation.

Wi-Fi / Smart Feature Issues

Markham's tech-savvy residents rely on app-connected washer features. We diagnose connectivity boards, firmware issues, and smart sensor failures on Samsung SmartThings and LG ThinQ washers to restore full functionality.
