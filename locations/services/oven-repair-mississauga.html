Common Oven Problems We Fix in Mississauga

From ovens that won't heat to digital displays that go dark, our Mississauga oven repair technicians diagnose and fix all faults quickly.

Oven Not Heating

The oven ignites or turns on but never reaches temperature. For electric ovens: a failed bake element or burnt-out oven sensor. For gas: a faulty igniter, gas valve, or thermocouple. We diagnose and replace the faulty component same-day.

Inaccurate Temperature

Dishes come out undercooked or burnt even at the correct setting. A worn oven temperature sensor or thermostat is usually to blame. We test calibration with precision equipment and replace sensors for accurate, consistent temperature.

Gas Burner Won't Ignite

Clicking sounds but the gas won't light, or clicking continues after lighting. Dirty or cracked igniter, clogged burner port, or faulty spark module. Common issue after cooking splatter. We clean and repair gas ignition systems safely.

Oven Door Won't Close Properly

The oven door doesn't seal fully, wasting energy and causing uneven cooking. Broken door hinges, worn door gasket, or damaged spring assembly. We replace hinges, seals, and springs to restore a proper door seal.

Control Panel or Display Not Working

Digital display is blank, buttons don't respond, or error codes appear. Usually a faulty control board or keypad membrane. We diagnose electronic faults and replace control boards with OEM-matched components.

Self-Clean Cycle Problems

Self-clean won't start, door locks and won't open, or oven won't reset after a cleaning cycle. Thermal fuse blown by the extreme heat, or a faulty door lock mechanism. We reset, replace, and restore self-clean function safely.
