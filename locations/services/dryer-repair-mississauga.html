Common Dryer Problems We Fix in Mississauga

From condo stackable units to suburban gas dryers, our Mississauga technicians diagnose and fix all dryer faults — usually in a single visit.

Dryer Not Heating

Clothes come out damp after a full cycle. Usually caused by a blown thermal fuse, faulty heating element, bad cycling thermostat, or a clogged vent. We test all components and fix most no-heat problems same-day for $150-$300.

Won't Start or No Power

Pressing start does nothing. Could be a faulty door switch, blown thermal fuse, bad start switch, or tripped circuit breaker. We test all electrical components and replace failed parts with OEM equivalents.

Drum Not Turning

Dryer runs but the drum stays still — clothes aren't tumbling. Indicates a broken drive belt, seized drum rollers, or faulty motor. We inspect the full drum assembly and restore rotation, typically within an hour.

Takes Too Long to Dry

Multiple cycles required to dry a load. Common in Mississauga condos with restricted vent runs. We clean the vent system end-to-end, check heating efficiency, and restore normal drying times.

Loud Grinding or Squealing

Grinding, thumping, or high-pitched squealing during operation. Worn drum rollers, frayed belt, or seized bearings are typical causes. We replace worn components so your dryer runs quietly again.

Overheating and Shutting Off

Dryer gets dangerously hot and cuts out mid-cycle. Usually a faulty high-limit thermostat or clogged vent — a fire risk. We clean venting and replace thermostats to restore safe operation.
