Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Brampton

From not cooling to water leaks and noisy operation, our Brampton technicians diagnose and repair all refrigerator faults — usually in a single visit.

Fridge Not Cooling

Food spoiling despite the fridge running. Commonly caused by dirty condenser coils, a failed evaporator fan, broken thermostat, or low refrigerant. We carry diagnostic equipment and parts for same-day resolution before more food is lost.

Freezer Not Freezing

Frozen food thawing or ice cream going soft. Often a defrost system failure — defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or defrost timer malfunction. We test the entire defrost circuit and replace the faulty component on-site.

Excessive Ice Build-Up

Ice accumulating on the evaporator coils blocks airflow and prevents cooling. This is a classic defrost system failure common in Brampton's heavy-use family fridges. We defrost the unit and fix the underlying component causing ice accumulation.

Water Leaking Inside or On Floor

Water pooling in the crisper drawer or on the kitchen floor. Causes include a blocked defrost drain tube, cracked water line to the ice maker, or a damaged door seal allowing warm air in. We clear blockages and replace seals or lines.

Ice Maker Not Working

Many Brampton families rely on their ice maker daily. Failed ice makers are caused by frozen water fill tubes, broken ice maker assemblies, or faulty water inlet valves. We restore ice production quickly with OEM replacement parts.

Fridge Making Loud Noises

Buzzing, clicking, or grinding sounds from the fridge. Noisy compressors, failing condenser fan motors, or rattling evaporator fans are the usual causes. We identify the exact source of the noise and repair it quietly.
