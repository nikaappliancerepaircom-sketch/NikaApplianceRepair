Common Oven Problems We Fix in Brampton

Heavy cooking in Brampton's busy family kitchens puts ovens and stoves through their paces. Our technicians fix all oven and range faults — most in a single visit.

Oven Not Heating

Oven won't reach temperature or heat at all. For electric ovens, a burned-out bake or broil element is the most common cause. For gas ovens, a faulty igniter or gas valve is typically responsible. We fix most no-heat oven problems same-day.

Gas Burner Won't Ignite

Clicking but no flame, or no response at all. Worn igniters and clogged burner ports are the usual culprits — especially in heavily-used Brampton family kitchens. We clean, repair, or replace igniters and burner assemblies to restore reliable ignition.

Oven Temperature Inaccurate

Baked goods burning or undercooking despite correct settings. A faulty oven temperature sensor or thermostat causes temperature drift. We calibrate and replace sensors to restore precise, reliable oven temperatures for consistent cooking results.

Oven Door Not Closing Properly

A loose or misaligned oven door wastes energy and causes uneven cooking. Worn door hinges, a damaged door gasket, or a broken door spring are common causes. We replace worn components and realign the door for a proper seal.

Control Panel or Display Not Working

Touchpad or digital display unresponsive or showing error codes. Control board failures require component-level diagnosis. We source OEM control boards for all major brands and install them with a 90-day warranty.

Self-Cleaning Cycle Failure

Oven locks or refuses to start, or won't unlock after a self-clean cycle. A faulty door lock motor, thermal fuse, or control board malfunction can cause this. We diagnose and restore safe self-cleaning function.
