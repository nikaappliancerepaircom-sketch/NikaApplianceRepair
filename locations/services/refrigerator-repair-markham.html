Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Markham

From Samsung French door defrost failures to LG compressor faults, our Markham technicians resolve most refrigerator problems in a single visit.

Not Cooling / Warm Fridge

Food is warm even though the fridge is running. Defrost system failure with ice blocking the evaporator coil is the #1 cause — extremely common in Samsung French door models. A failed start relay or compressor fault is also possible. We diagnose and fix on the first visit.

Freezer Works, Fridge Doesn't

A working freezer with a warm fresh food compartment usually indicates a blocked or failed evaporator fan motor, or ice buildup restricting airflow through the evaporator. Common in Samsung and LG side-by-side and French door units prevalent in Markham.

Ice Maker Not Working

No ice, slow ice production, or the ice maker is making a grinding noise. Frozen fill tubes, a faulty water inlet valve, failed ice maker module, or a frozen defrost drain are common causes. We restore full ice maker function same-day.

Water Leaking Inside or Under Fridge

Puddles inside the crisper drawer or on the floor typically point to a blocked defrost drain line or a failed door gasket allowing warm, humid air in. We clear drain lines and replace gaskets to stop leaks and prevent floor damage.

Fridge Running Constantly

A fridge that never cycles off drives up hydro bills and signals a problem. Worn door gaskets letting warm air in, a failed defrost heater, or a faulty temperature sensor are typical causes. We restore proper temperature cycling for efficient operation.

LG Linear Compressor Fault

LG French door and bottom-freezer refrigerators in Markham sometimes fail due to the linear compressor design. Our technicians are trained specifically on LG linear compressor diagnosis and replacement, avoiding misdiagnosis of a repairable fault as a compressor replacement.
