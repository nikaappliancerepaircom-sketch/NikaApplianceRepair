Common Stove & Range Repair Problems We Fix in Little Portugal

Narrow home layouts with basement laundry are typical. Some older homes have limited electrical panel capacity. Basement access for washer and dryer repairs is routine.

Burner Not Igniting

Gas burner clicking but not lighting, or electric coil not heating. Clogged igniter ports, failed igniter switch, or burnt-out burner element are common causes. We clean ports and replace igniters.

Burner Heating Unevenly

Hot spots or cool zones on the cooking surface. Partially clogged gas ports or a failing electric element cause uneven heating. We clean burner systems and replace elements as needed.

Stove Control Knob Broken

Knob loose, cracked, or not controlling the burner properly. We source manufacturer-matched replacement knobs and repair the underlying valve or switch if needed.

Gas Smell from Stove

Smell of gas near the range at any time. This requires immediate attention. We check all gas connections, valve seals, and burner fittings for leaks. Do not use the stove until this is resolved.

Electric Coil Burner Broken

Coil element not glowing, sparking, or broken in two. Element or drip bowl replacements are quick repairs. We stock common coil sizes for same-day replacement.

Stove Control Board Fault

Display errors, buttons not responding, or digital controls behaving erratically. Control board failures are often repairable without full replacement. We diagnose and advise the most cost-effective fix.

What should I prepare before the stove technician arrives in Little Portugal?

For a gas stove, start with safety: if you have noticed any gas smell, turn all knobs to OFF, close the gas shut-off behind the range, open windows, and let us know when booking. For a standard repair, turn the stove off and let it cool. Remove burner grates and burner caps from the cooktop so the technician can access the burner heads and igniter electrodes directly. Leave any visible residue or clogging in place so the technician can assess the condition — it is diagnostic information. Pull the range slightly away from the wall to give access to the rear panel and gas connection. Write down which specific burner or oven function has failed: front left burner not lighting, oven not heating, multiple burners not sparking. Note the symptom precisely — clicking but no flame is different from no spark at all, and both are different from a burner that lights but runs weak. For apartment units with electric ranges, note any error codes from the display before resetting the unit, and allow the surface to cool completely before the visit. Locate the model number inside the oven door frame.
