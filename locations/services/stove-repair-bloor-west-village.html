Common Stove & Range Repair Problems We Fix in Bloor West Village

Heritage home layouts include narrow galley kitchens with limited appliance clearance. Basement laundry is typical in bungalows.

Burner Not Igniting

Gas burner clicking but not lighting, or electric coil not heating. Clogged igniter ports, failed igniter switch, or burnt-out burner element are common causes. We clean ports and replace igniters.

Burner Heating Unevenly

Hot spots or cool zones on the cooking surface. Partially clogged gas ports or a failing electric element cause uneven heating. We clean burner systems and replace elements as needed.

Stove Control Knob Broken

Knob loose, cracked, or not controlling the burner properly. We source manufacturer-matched replacement knobs and repair the underlying valve or switch if needed.

Gas Smell from Stove

Smell of gas near the range at any time. This requires immediate attention. We check all gas connections, valve seals, and burner fittings for leaks. Do not use the stove until this is resolved.

Electric Coil Burner Broken

Coil element not glowing, sparking, or broken in two. Element or drip bowl replacements are quick repairs. We stock common coil sizes for same-day replacement.

Stove Control Board Fault

Display errors, buttons not responding, or digital controls behaving erratically. Control board failures are often repairable without full replacement. We diagnose and advise the most cost-effective fix.

What should I prepare before the stove technician arrives in Bloor West Village?

A few quick steps make the visit faster and safer. First, turn off the stove completely — for gas ranges, turn all knobs to OFF; if you smelled gas at any point, turn off the gas supply at the shut-off valve behind the range and open a window. Clear the burner area: remove all pots, pans, and any items stored on or around the range. Write down which specific burner or function is not working — front left, rear right, oven bake, oven broil — and whether the problem is no heat, no ignition, error code, or something else.
