Common Dryer Problems We Fix in Brampton

Brampton's large households put dryers through heavy daily use. Our technicians diagnose and repair all dryer faults — usually in a single visit.

Dryer Not Heating

Heavy daily use in Brampton's large family homes strains thermal fuses and heating elements. Clothes come out damp even after a full cycle. We test every heating component and fix most no-heat problems same-day for $150-$300.

Won't Start or No Power

Pressing start does nothing. Could be a faulty door switch, blown thermal fuse, bad start switch, or tripped circuit breaker. We test all electrical components and replace failed parts with OEM equivalents.

Drum Not Turning

Dryer runs but the drum stays still. High-volume use in large households accelerates belt and roller wear. We inspect the full drum assembly and restore rotation — typically within an hour.

Takes Too Long to Dry

Multiple loads per day in Brampton homes leads to rapid lint accumulation in vent ducts. We clean the vent system end-to-end, check heating efficiency, and restore normal drying times so your household routine stays on track.

Loud Grinding or Squealing

Worn drum rollers, frayed belts, or seized bearings cause thumping and squealing — common in heavily-used machines. We replace worn components so your dryer runs quietly again without interrupting the household.

Overheating and Shutting Off

Dryer gets dangerously hot and cuts out mid-cycle. Usually a faulty high-limit thermostat or clogged vent — a real fire hazard. We clean venting and replace thermostats to restore safe, reliable operation.
