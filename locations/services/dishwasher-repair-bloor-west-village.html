Common Dishwasher Repair Problems We Fix in Bloor West Village

Heritage home layouts include narrow galley kitchens with limited appliance clearance. Basement laundry is typical in bungalows.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning Dishes

Food residue remaining after a full cycle. Clogged spray arms, a failing wash pump, or low water temperature are most common. We clean and repair the spray and pump system.

Dishwasher Not Draining

Standing water in the bottom of the tub after the cycle. Clogged drain filter, failed drain pump, or blocked drain hose are typical. We clear blockages and replace pumps on-site.

Dishwasher Leaking Water

Water on the kitchen floor during or after a cycle. Door gasket deterioration, loose hose connections, or a damaged pump seal cause leaks. We replace seals and inspect all water connections.

Dishwasher Not Starting

No response when you press the start button. Door latch failure, control board issue, or faulty door switch are common causes. We run a full diagnostic and repair the specific failure.

Dishwasher Making Loud Noise

Grinding or buzzing during operation. A worn wash pump bearing, debris in the pump, or a failing motor causes most noise complaints. We inspect the pump and motor assembly.

Dishwasher Error Codes

Error codes that do not clear after a reset. These codes indicate specific component failures in the control system, sensors, or pump. We decode and fix the root cause.
