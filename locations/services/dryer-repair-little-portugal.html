Common Dryer Repair Problems We Fix in Little Portugal

Narrow home layouts with basement laundry are typical. Some older homes have limited electrical panel capacity. Basement access for washer and dryer repairs is routine.

Dryer Not Heating

Clothes coming out damp after a full cycle. Blown thermal fuse, failed heating element, or a bad cycling thermostat are most common. We carry heating elements for all major brands.

Dryer Not Turning

Drum stationary when the cycle starts. A broken drive belt is the most frequent cause, followed by a failed motor or idler pulley. Belt replacements take 30 to 60 minutes.

Dryer Takes Too Long

Multiple cycles needed to dry a single load. Clogged exhaust duct or a failing heating element are almost always responsible. We clean ducts and replace parts in one visit.

Dryer Making Loud Noise

Squealing, thumping, or grinding during operation. Worn drum glides, damaged drum seal, or failing drum bearings are typical. We replace worn components before they cause further damage.

Dryer Not Starting

No response when the start button is pressed. Door switch, start switch, motor, or thermal fuse failures cause this. We test the full electrical circuit and replace the faulty component.

Dryer Overheating

Drum excessively hot or the unit shutting off mid-cycle. A blocked exhaust duct or failed high-limit thermostat causes overheating. This is a fire risk and should be addressed promptly.

What are the most common dryer problems in Little Portugal homes?

Gas igniter failure is the most common dryer problem in Little Portugal on older Whirlpool gas units. The silicon carbide igniter weakens over 7-10 years and loses the ability to glow hot enough to open the gas valve. The result is a dryer that tumbles and heats briefly then goes cold, or never heats at all. Igniter replacement costs $150-$220 and is usually done same-day. The second most common problem is drum bearing and drum support roller wear: older Whirlpool rear-drum-support units develop a loud squealing or thumping as the felt drum bearing and rear rollers wear down. Drum support roller and bearing replacement at $200-$350 eliminates the noise and extends the machine life considerably. Third is lint-clogged vent ducting causing thermal fuse trips, which we encounter on nearly every Little Portugal basement laundry call.
