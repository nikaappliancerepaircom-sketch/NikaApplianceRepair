Common Washer Problems We Fix in Mississauga

From not draining to leaking all over the floor, our Mississauga technicians have seen every washing machine fault and fix most in a single visit.

Washer Won't Drain

Water stays in the drum after the cycle ends. A clogged drain pump filter, faulty pump motor, or kinked drain hose are typical culprits. Common in Mississauga homes with hard water residue clogging filters. Fixed same-day for $150-$280.

Won't Spin or Agitate

Clothes come out soaking wet because the drum won't spin. Usually caused by a worn drive belt, faulty lid switch (top-load), broken coupling, or motor control board issue. We test and replace failed components on-site.

Leaking Water

Water pooling on the laundry room floor is a common emergency. Door boot seals on front-loaders, cracked hoses, and worn pump seals are typical causes. We identify the source and replace seals and hoses to stop leaks fast.

Excessive Vibration and Noise

Banging, thumping, or intense vibration — especially during the spin cycle. Worn drum bearings, broken shock absorbers, or uneven feet are common causes. We restore smooth, quiet operation.

Won't Start or No Power

Nothing happens when you press start. Faulty lid switch, failed door latch (front-load), bad timer, or control board failure. We test all electrical components and replace only what's failed.

Not Cleaning Clothes Properly

Clothes come out still dirty or with soap residue. Often related to a faulty water inlet valve restricting fill, blocked dispenser, or incorrect water temperature. We restore full wash performance.
