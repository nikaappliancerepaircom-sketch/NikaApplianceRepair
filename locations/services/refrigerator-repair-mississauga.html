Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Mississauga

From not cooling to ice maker failures, our Mississauga technicians diagnose and repair all refrigerator faults with same-day urgency.

Not Cooling or Warm Fridge

The most urgent fridge problem — food spoils fast. Usually caused by a faulty evaporator fan, failed thermostat, dirty condenser coils, or a failing compressor. We diagnose the root cause and fix it fast to save your groceries.

Freezer Not Freezing

The fridge section is fine but the freezer won't maintain temperature. Often a defrost system failure (heater, thermostat, or timer), faulty evaporator fan, or sealed system problem. We restore proper freezer operation same-day in most cases.

Water Leaking Inside or Outside

Puddles inside the fridge or water on your kitchen floor. Blocked defrost drain, cracked drain pan, faulty water inlet valve, or ice maker line issues are typical causes. Common in Mississauga units with hard water deposits blocking drains.

Loud or Unusual Noises

Clicking, buzzing, rattling, or loud humming from your fridge. Could be a failing compressor, dirty condenser fan, loose drain pan, or a malfunctioning ice maker. We identify the source and eliminate the noise.

Ice Maker Not Working

No ice or small misshapen cubes. Frozen water supply line, failed ice maker assembly, clogged water filter, or faulty water inlet valve. We repair Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, and GE ice maker systems.

Fridge Running Constantly

Motor never shuts off, energy bills spiking. Usually dirty condenser coils, damaged door gaskets allowing warm air in, or a thermostat calibration issue. We clean, seal, and recalibrate to restore efficient operation.
