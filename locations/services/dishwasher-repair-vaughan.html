Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Vaughan

From Miele error codes to Bosch drain failures and Thermador leaks, our Vaughan technicians diagnose and repair all dishwasher faults with specialist precision.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning

Premium dishwashers in Vaughan that leave dishes with food residue typically have clogged spray arms, filter blockages, or a failing wash pump. On Miele models, a blocked spray arm nozzle or worn wash impeller is the most common cause. We clean, restore, or replace as needed.

Dishwasher Not Draining

Standing water after a cycle in your Vaughan kitchen is an urgent problem — especially with hardwood or tile floors nearby. Bosch's AquaStop sensor can trigger, preventing drain. We clear blockages, replace drain pumps, and reset protection systems correctly.

Dishwasher Leaking

A leak from a premium dishwasher in Vaughan's designer kitchen is alarming. Miele, Bosch, and Thermador units can activate flood protection mode, preventing further damage. We identify the leak source — door seal, pump, or hose — and repair it with OEM parts.

Error Codes and Fault Lights

Miele, Bosch, and Thermador dishwashers display specific fault codes. Our technicians use manufacturer diagnostic tools to interpret these codes correctly and address the actual component failure, not just clear the error and hope it doesn't return.

Dishes Not Drying

Thermador StarDry, Miele's AutoOpen drying, and Bosch's CrystalDry systems all have unique mechanisms. When dishes come out wet, the fault is specific to each brand's drying system. We diagnose the actual cause rather than applying generic solutions.

Dishwasher Making Noise

In Vaughan's open-plan kitchens, a noisy dishwasher is disruptive. Miele and Bosch units are engineered for near-silent operation — unusual noise from the pump motor, spray arm, or door seal indicates a fault that we trace and fix precisely.
