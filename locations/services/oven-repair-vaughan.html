Common Oven Problems We Fix in Vaughan

From Wolf range igniter failures to Miele steam oven faults, our Vaughan technicians handle premium and standard appliance repairs with equal precision.

Oven Not Heating

A Wolf or Miele oven that won't reach temperature has specific failure modes — probe sensors, convection fan motors, and electronic ignition modules all require brand-specific diagnosis. We identify the exact failed part and replace it with OEM components.

Gas Burner Won't Ignite

Wolf and Thermador professional ranges have sealed burner systems with premium ignition assemblies. Clicking without flame, or no response, indicates a worn igniter electrode, clogged burner port, or ignition module failure. We restore reliable ignition with genuine parts.

Induction Cooktop Not Working

Bosch, Miele, and LG induction cooktops in Vaughan's modern kitchens require specialist diagnosis. Failed induction coil modules, control board faults, or thermal protection trips are the usual causes. We diagnose at the component level to minimize replacement cost.

Oven Temperature Inaccurate

For serious cooks in Vaughan's kitchen-proud households, temperature precision matters. A faulty oven probe or control thermostat causes temperature drift. We calibrate and replace temperature management components to restore accurate, consistent baking results.

Control Panel / Display Failure

Touch-screen control panels on Miele, Bosch, and Samsung ovens can fail from software faults or hardware failures. We diagnose whether the touchpad, control board, or software is at fault and carry OEM control boards for the most common Vaughan models.

Convection Fan Not Working

A convection oven without a functioning fan produces uneven results. A failed fan motor or motor mounting issue is the usual cause. We replace convection fan assemblies for all brands with OEM parts and test calibration before leaving your home.
