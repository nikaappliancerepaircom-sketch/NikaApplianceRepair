Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Markham

From leaking to not cleaning properly, our Markham technicians diagnose and fix most dishwasher faults on the first visit.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning Properly

Dishes come out with food residue or a white film. York Region's hard water deposits mineral scale on spray arm nozzles and the filter assembly, reducing wash pressure. We clean the system and replace worn spray arms and water valves to restore full cleaning performance.

Not Draining After Cycle

Standing water at the bottom of the tub after the cycle ends. A blocked drain filter, failed drain pump motor, or kinked drain hose are typical causes. We clear blockages and replace failed drain components same-day.

Leaking Water

Water pooling under the dishwasher can quickly damage Markham kitchens with hardwood flooring. A worn door gasket, failed pump seal, or loose hose connection are common culprits. We identify the leak source and seal it same-day.

Not Starting / No Power

Nothing happens when you press start. A faulty door latch preventing the safety interlock from engaging, a blown thermal fuse, or a failed control board are typical causes. We test all electrical components and replace only what has failed.

Dishes Not Drying

Dishes come out wet even with heated dry selected. A failed heating element (on non-Bosch models), broken vent, or faulty high-limit thermostat are common causes. We restore drying performance and verify rinse aid dispenser function.

Loud Noise During Wash Cycle

Grinding, rattling, or thumping sounds during the wash cycle. A worn wash motor bearing, foreign object caught in the pump, or loose spray arm are typical causes. We disassemble, clear, and replace worn components to restore quiet operation.
