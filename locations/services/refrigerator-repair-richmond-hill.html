Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Richmond Hill

From a warm fridge to an overflowing ice maker, our Richmond Hill technicians resolve most refrigerator faults on the first visit.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Food is warming despite the fridge running. Defrost system failure, evaporator fan failure, or a compressor issue are the most common causes. In Richmond Hill's larger homes running high-capacity units continuously, compressor and sealed system calls are more frequent. We diagnose accurately before recommending any repair.

Freezer Works, Fresh Food Compartment Doesn't

A fully working freezer paired with a warm fridge section indicates a blocked evaporator fan or ice buildup on the evaporator coil. We defrost the coil, test the defrost heater and thermostat, and replace failed components on the first visit.

Ice Maker Not Working

No ice, slow production, or unusual noise from the ice maker. Frozen fill tubes, a clogged water inlet valve, or a failed ice maker module are common causes. Richmond Hill's hard water can accelerate inlet valve blockage. We restore ice production same-day.

Water Pooling Under or Inside Fridge

Standing water inside the crisper drawer or puddles on the kitchen floor. A blocked defrost drain line, cracked drain pan, or failed door gasket are typical culprits. We clear drain lines and replace gaskets to prevent floor damage in Richmond Hill's finished kitchens.

Refrigerator Running Constantly

Never cycling off drives up hydro costs and signals a problem. Worn door gaskets letting in warm air, a failing defrost system, or a faulty temperature control are common causes in Richmond Hill's larger fridges that run continuously under heavy household load.

Noisy Refrigerator

Rattling, buzzing, or grinding noises. Loose condenser fan blade, failing evaporator fan motor bearing, or a compressor nearing end of life are typical causes. We diagnose the noise source accurately and give you an honest repair vs. replace assessment.
