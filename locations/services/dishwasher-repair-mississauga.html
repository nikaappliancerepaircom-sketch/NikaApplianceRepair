Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Mississauga

Many dishwasher problems in Mississauga are made worse by hard water. Our technicians address both the mechanical fault and the underlying water quality impact.

Not Cleaning Dishes Properly

Dishes come out dirty, cloudy, or with white residue. In Mississauga, this often combines a mechanical fault (clogged spray arms, weak pump) with hard water scale. We unclog spray arms, check water pressure, and descale internal components.

Dishwasher Leaking

Water on the kitchen floor or pooling under the dishwasher — an urgent call to prevent floor and cabinet damage. Cracked door gasket, loose hose connection, faulty pump seal, or a stuck float switch. We identify the source and stop the leak fast.

Won't Drain

Standing water at the bottom of the dishwasher after a cycle. Clogged drain hose, blocked filter, faulty drain pump, or garbage disposal connection issue. We clear blockages and replace failed drain components.

Won't Start or No Power

Pressing start does nothing, or the control panel is blank. Faulty door latch (dishwasher won't run with an open door signal), tripped thermal fuse, bad control board, or a wiring issue. We test all circuits and replace the faulty component.

Not Drying Dishes

Dishes are wet at the end of the cycle even on heated dry setting. A failed heating element, faulty high-limit thermostat, or defective vent fan are typical causes. We restore proper drying function with correct OEM components.

Loud Grinding or Unusual Noises

Grinding, rattling, or loud humming during the wash cycle. Debris caught in the pump impeller, worn spray arm bearings, or a failing circulation pump motor. We remove debris and replace worn pump components.
