Common Oven Problems We Fix in Markham

From a gas burner that won't ignite to an oven that won't reach temperature, our Markham technicians fix most stove and oven faults in a single visit.

Oven Not Heating to Temperature

The oven turns on but never reaches the set temperature, or takes much longer than normal. A failed bake element (electric), faulty oven igniter (gas), or defective temperature sensor are the most common causes. We test and replace on-site in most cases.

Gas Burner Won't Ignite

The click of the igniter sparks but the burner doesn't light — or won't click at all. A clogged burner cap, failed igniter module, or faulty spark electrode are typical culprits. With heavy cooking habits common in Markham's diverse households, burner igniters see heavy use.

Broiler Not Working

The oven bakes fine but the broiler produces no heat. The broil element (electric) or broiler burner and igniter (gas) are usually at fault. We replace failed broil components same-day to restore full oven functionality.

Oven Door Not Sealing

Excess heat escaping from the oven door causes longer cooking times and uneven results. A worn door gasket or broken door hinge spring is typically the cause. We replace door seals and hinges on-site to restore efficient, even cooking.

Error Codes on Digital Display

Smart ranges and wall ovens in Markham's newer homes display error codes (F1, F2, F3, etc.) indicating sensor or board failures. Our technicians interpret codes for all major brands and carry common control board modules for same-day resolution.

Self-Cleaning Function Failure

Self-clean cycles that fail to start, or ovens that lock up and won't unlock after a clean cycle, are common on Samsung and LG ranges. We reset control boards and replace faulty door lock motors to restore safe operation.
