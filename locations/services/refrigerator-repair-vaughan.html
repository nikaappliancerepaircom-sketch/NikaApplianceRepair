Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Vaughan

From Sub-Zero compressor issues to Miele ice maker failures, our Vaughan technicians diagnose and repair all refrigerator faults with precision.

Fridge Not Cooling

In Sub-Zero units, both the primary and secondary compressors can fail independently — a nuance most general technicians miss. In Miele and Bosch, sealed system faults and evaporator fan issues are common. We identify the precise fault and repair it correctly.

Freezer Not Freezing

A failed defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or defrost timer causes ice build-up that blocks airflow. This is common even in premium brands. We test the complete defrost circuit and replace the specific failed component.

Ice Maker Not Working

Vaughan households entertain frequently, making ice maker reliability important. Frozen water fill tubes, failed ice maker assemblies, or blocked water lines are common causes. We restore ice production quickly with OEM parts for all brands.

Water Leaking Inside or Below

A blocked defrost drain tube or damaged water line to the ice maker causes water pooling. In Vaughan's luxury kitchens with custom flooring and cabinetry, a leaking fridge demands immediate attention to prevent costly water damage.

Fridge Making Unusual Noises

Buzzing, clicking, rattling, or humming from a premium refrigerator in Vaughan's open-plan kitchen is disruptive. We identify whether the compressor, condenser fan, evaporator fan, or ice maker is the noise source and repair it precisely.

Temperature Fluctuations

A refrigerator that can't maintain consistent temperature risks food safety. Faulty thermistors, control board malfunctions, or a failing compressor cause temperature swings. We calibrate and repair the temperature management system to precise manufacturer specifications.
