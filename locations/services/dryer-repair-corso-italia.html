Common Dryer Repair Problems We Fix in Corso Italia

Family-size appliances are the norm. Some older homes have 60-amp electrical panels. Multi-generational households run appliances heavily and value reliability.

Dryer Not Heating

Clothes coming out damp after a full cycle. Blown thermal fuse, failed heating element, or a bad cycling thermostat are most common. We carry heating elements for all major brands.

Dryer Not Turning

Drum stationary when the cycle starts. A broken drive belt is the most frequent cause, followed by a failed motor or idler pulley. Belt replacements take 30 to 60 minutes.

Dryer Takes Too Long

Multiple cycles needed to dry a single load. Clogged exhaust duct or a failing heating element are almost always responsible. We clean ducts and replace parts in one visit.

Dryer Making Loud Noise

Squealing, thumping, or grinding during operation. Worn drum glides, damaged drum seal, or failing drum bearings are typical. We replace worn components before they cause further damage.

Dryer Not Starting

No response when the start button is pressed. Door switch, start switch, motor, or thermal fuse failures cause this. We test the full electrical circuit and replace the faulty component.

Dryer Overheating

Drum excessively hot or the unit shutting off mid-cycle. A blocked exhaust duct or failed high-limit thermostat causes overheating. This is a fire risk and should be addressed promptly.

What are the most common dryer problems in Corso Italia homes?

Gas igniter failure is the most frequent dryer call we receive from Corso Italia. The older Maytag and Whirlpool gas dryers in these 1950s-1970s homes use silicon carbide igniters that become brittle over time and crack or fail to glow hot enough to open the gas valve. The symptom is a dryer that tumbles normally but produces no heat. Igniter replacement costs $150-$220 and is almost always a same-day fix. The second most common issue is vent duct fouling from lint: long basement vent runs trap lint at every elbow, causing overheating and repeated thermal fuse trips. We clean the duct at every gas dryer call in Corso Italia to prevent recurrence. The third most common repair is drum belt failure on older machines — the belt hardens and cracks after 10+ years, leaving the drum stationary while the motor runs. Belt replacement costs $120-$180.
