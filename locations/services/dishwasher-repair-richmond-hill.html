Common Dishwasher Problems We Fix in Richmond Hill

From dishes with hard water spots to a machine that won't drain, our Richmond Hill technicians resolve most faults in a single visit.

Dishes Coming Out Cloudy or Spotty

A white film or cloudiness on dishes is a hallmark of York Region's hard water. Clogged spray arm nozzles reduce wash pressure, and mineral scale on the heating element impairs drying. We descale the system, clear nozzles, and test water temperature to restore sparkling clean results.

Not Draining After Cycle

Standing water in the tub after the cycle. A clogged drain filter loaded with food debris (especially common after cooking heavy meals), a failed drain pump motor, or a blocked drain hose are typical causes. We clear blockages and replace failed drain components same-day.

Leaking Water

Water on the kitchen floor is an urgent concern in Richmond Hill homes where open-plan kitchens flow into finished living spaces. A worn door gasket, cracked pump seal, or loose hose connection are common causes. We identify and seal the leak on the first visit.

Not Starting or No Power

Nothing happens when you select a cycle and press start. A faulty door latch (the most common cause — the safety interlock must engage), a tripped thermal fuse, or a failed control board are typical culprits. We test all electrical components and replace only what has failed.

Dishes Not Getting Clean

Food residue remaining on dishes after a full cycle. Clogged spray arms from scale buildup, a weak pump due to bearing wear, or insufficient water temperature from a failed heating element are common causes. We address the root issue, not just the symptom.

Loud or Unusual Noises

Grinding, humming, or rattling during the wash cycle. Foreign objects caught in the pump (glass shards, small utensils), a worn wash motor bearing, or a cracked spray arm are common causes in high-use Richmond Hill dishwashers. We disassemble, clear, and replace worn components.
