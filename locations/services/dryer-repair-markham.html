Common Dryer Problems We Fix in Markham

From no heat to error codes on smart dryers, our Markham technicians diagnose and fix most dryer faults in a single visit.

Dryer Not Heating

The drum spins but clothes stay damp. On electric dryers this usually means a blown heating element or failed thermal fuse. On gas dryers, a faulty igniter or gas valve solenoid is typical. We test and replace on-site — most resolved in under an hour.

Takes Too Long to Dry

Markham homes often have longer vent runs, especially in townhomes and semi-detached properties. Lint buildup in the exhaust duct is the most common cause of slow drying — and a fire risk. We clear vent blockages and inspect the full exhaust path.

Dryer Won't Start

Nothing happens when you press start. A faulty door latch, broken thermal fuse, failed start switch, or blown control board are typical causes. Our technicians test all electrical components and replace only what has failed.

Drum Not Spinning

The dryer runs and heats but the drum doesn't turn. A snapped drum belt is the most common cause — straightforward to replace. We also check idler pulleys and drum glides that wear out in large-capacity units popular in Markham homes.

Overheating / Shutting Off

Dryer runs for a few minutes then cuts out, or clothes come out scorching hot. A blocked lint filter or restricted exhaust duct causes the high-limit thermostat to trip. We clean the duct and replace thermostats to restore safe operation.

Smart Dryer Error Codes

Samsung and LG smart dryers in Markham display codes like dE, HE, or FE before stopping. We use brand-specific diagnostic tools to decode faults in moisture sensors, temperature sensors, and control boards for accurate, first-visit fixes.
