What Are the Most Common Appliance Problems?

Our licensed technician team provides insured appliance repair services across Toronto, GTA, and Ontario, handling hundreds of urgent calls monthly with upfront pricing and professional diagnostics. Here are the most common appliance issues we resolve quickly with guaranteed satisfaction and quality workmanship.

❄️

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Compressor failure, thermostat malfunction, or blocked vents. We diagnose and fix these issues fast to prevent food spoilage.

🌊

Washer Won't Drain

Clogged pumps, faulty drain hoses, or lid switch problems. Our technicians carry replacement parts for same-day fixes.

🔥

Dryer Not Heating

Heating element burnout, thermal fuse issues, or gas valve failure. We restore proper drying function quickly.

💧

Dishwasher Leaking

Door gasket wear, spray arm clogs, or pump seal damage. We stop leaks and prevent water damage to your home.

🌡️

Oven Temperature Issues

Calibration problems, thermostat failure, or heating element defects. We ensure accurate cooking temperatures.

🔧

Stove Burner Problems

Igniter failure, gas valve issues, or electrical connection problems. Professional repairs ensure safe operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth it to fix an appliance? +

Most appliances are worth repairing if they're less than 10 years old and the repair cost is less than 50% of replacement cost. Our technicians provide honest assessments and will tell you if replacement makes more sense.

Which is better repair or replace? +

Generally, repair if: the appliance is less than 8 years old, repair costs less than 50% of new, or it's a high-end model. Replace if: it's over 15 years old, repairs are frequent, or energy efficiency is poor.

What is the warranty on your services? +

We offer a comprehensive 90-day warranty on all repairs. This covers both parts and labor. If the same issue occurs within 90 days, we'll fix it free of charge.

What is the best way to maintain my appliances? +

Regular cleaning, following manufacturer guidelines, not overloading, and scheduling annual maintenance checks. We offer maintenance services to keep your appliances running efficiently.

What should I do if my appliance breaks down? +

First, unplug the appliance for safety. Check basic issues like power supply or circuit breakers. If the problem persists, call us at 437-747-6737 for same-day service. Don't attempt complex repairs yourself.
