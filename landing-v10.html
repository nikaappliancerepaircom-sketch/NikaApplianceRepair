Everything You Need to Know

Straight answers. No runaround.

In most cases, we can schedule a same-day or next-day visit. After you call or submit our online form, we confirm your appointment within 60 minutes. We serve Toronto and the full GTA, so availability is usually excellent.

If our technician diagnoses your appliance and cannot repair it — or if the repair cost isn't worth it — you pay nothing. We don't charge a diagnostic fee on failed repairs. Your time and trust matter more than a quick service call fee.

Our 90-day warranty covers both the parts we install and the labour performed. If the same issue returns within 90 days, we come back and fix it at no additional cost. This warranty is included on every repair — no exceptions.

We repair all major brands including Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, Bosch, GE, Frigidaire, Maytag, KitchenAid, Electrolux, Miele, Sub-Zero, Amana, and more. If you don't see your brand listed, just call us — chances are we service it.

Yes. We use original manufacturer parts or OEM-equivalent parts that meet manufacturer specifications. We never use cheap aftermarket parts that shorten your appliance's life. Quality parts are why our repairs last.

Most repairs start from $150 for standard issues. We always provide an upfront, written quote before any work begins — so you know exactly what you'll pay. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges on your invoice.
