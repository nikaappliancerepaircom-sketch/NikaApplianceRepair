Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your appliance repair.

Most appliance repairs range from $120 to $350, depending on the appliance type, brand, and parts required. We provide a firm, upfront quote before any work begins — no surprises, no hidden fees. The diagnostic visit fee is waived if you proceed with the repair.
Yes! We offer same-day service across Toronto and the GTA for most appliance types. Call us before noon and we will typically have a technician at your door the same day. Availability may vary by location and current demand.
We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Bosch, Frigidaire, Maytag, KitchenAid, Electrolux, Kenmore, Miele, and Amana — plus many more. If you do not see your brand listed, call us and we will let you know if we can help.
We charge a small diagnostic fee to visit your home and assess the appliance. This fee is waived entirely when you approve the repair. If we cannot diagnose the issue or you decline the repair, the diagnostic fee is all you pay — and if we cannot fix it, there is no charge at all.
All repairs are backed by a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labour. If the same issue returns within 90 days, we come back and fix it at no additional cost to you. We stand behind every repair we perform.
We service Toronto, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Markham, Brampton, Mississauga, East Gwillimbury, Newmarket, Aurora, Barrie, King City, and Woodbridge — covering the full Greater Toronto Area. Not sure if we cover your area? Call us at 437-524-1053.
Most repairs are completed on the first visit within 1 to 3 hours. If a specific part needs to be ordered, we will schedule a follow-up visit as soon as the part arrives — usually within 1 to 3 business days.
We operate on a strict No Fix, No Fee policy. If our technician determines that your appliance is beyond economical repair, or if we are unable to fix the problem, you will not be charged for the visit. We will also give you an honest recommendation on whether repair or replacement makes more financial sense.
