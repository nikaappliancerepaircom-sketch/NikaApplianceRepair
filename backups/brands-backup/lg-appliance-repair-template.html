LG Appliance Repair FAQs

Common questions about our LG repair service

How much does LG appliance repair cost?

LG repair costs vary by model and issue. Diagnostic fee is $119 (waived with repair). Most repairs range from $200-$500 including parts and labor. We provide upfront pricing before starting any work.

Do you use genuine LG parts?

Yes! We use authentic LG replacement parts to ensure proper fit, function, and longevity. Genuine parts also help maintain any existing warranty coverage.

Can you fix LG Smart ThinQ appliances?

Absolutely! Our technicians are trained on LG's Smart ThinQ technology. We troubleshoot WiFi connectivity, app issues, and smart feature malfunctions.

How quickly can you repair my LG appliance?

We offer same-day service for most LG repairs in Toronto. Our trucks carry common LG parts, allowing many repairs to be completed on the first visit.

Is my older LG appliance worth repairing?

LG appliances are built to last! If your appliance is under 10 years old and repair costs less than 50% of replacement, repair is usually worthwhile.

What areas do you service?

We service all of Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area including North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, East York, and surrounding regions.
