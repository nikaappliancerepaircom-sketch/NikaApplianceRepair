LG Appliance Repair FAQs

LG appliance repair costs vary by model and issue. Our diagnostic fee is $119 (waived with repair). Most LG repairs range from $200-$500 including parts and labor. Complex repairs like linear compressor replacement may cost more. We provide upfront pricing before any work begins.

Yes! We use authentic LG replacement parts whenever possible. Genuine parts ensure proper fit, function, and longevity. They also help maintain any existing warranty. If LG parts aren't immediately available, we can use high-quality OEM alternatives with your approval.

Absolutely! Our technicians are trained on LG's Smart ThinQ technology. We can troubleshoot WiFi connectivity issues, app problems, and smart feature malfunctions. We also use Smart Diagnosis when available to quickly identify problems.

We offer same-day service for most LG repairs in Toronto and GTA. Our technicians carry common LG parts, allowing many repairs to be completed on the first visit. For less common parts, we can usually get them within 1-2 business days.

We fix all LG error codes including: OE (drain error), UE (unbalanced load), LE (motor error), Er FF/Er IF (fan errors), d80/d90 (vent blockage), and many more. Our technicians have comprehensive training on LG diagnostic codes.

LG appliances are built to last, so repair is often worthwhile if under 10 years old. As a rule, if repair costs less than 50% of replacement, it's worth fixing. We'll give honest advice about whether repair or replacement makes more sense for your situation.
