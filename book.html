Booking Questions Answered

How quickly can you come after I book?

We offer same-day service for most bookings in Toronto and GTA. If you book in the morning, we can often arrive within 2-4 hours. Next-day appointments are always available.

What happens after I submit the booking form?

You'll receive instant confirmation via email and SMS with your appointment details. Our dispatch team will assign a technician, and you'll get updates when they're on the way.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, absolutely. Just call us at 437-747-6737 or reply to your confirmation email. We recommend giving at least 2 hours notice for changes.

Do I need to be home during the repair?

Yes, someone 18+ needs to be present during the service call to approve the quote and provide access to the appliance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), debit cards, and e-transfers. Payment is due after the repair is completed and tested.

What areas do you service?

We serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, and surrounding GTA communities. Check our Locations page for your specific area.

Prefer to Talk to Someone?

Our friendly customer service team is ready to help you book your appointment over the phone or answer any questions you have about our services.
